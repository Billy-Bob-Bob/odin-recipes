Spaghetti alla carbonara

A quick and easy carbonara recipe for your special occasions.

Recipe

When I was young, my mother always told me to start every recipe with

long and unrelated story of my life. Back in 1965...

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 90g guanciale
  • 3 egg yolks
  • pecorino
  • black pepper

Method

  1. Cook guanciale until crispy
  2. Boil spaghetti
  3. Mix the egg yolks with pecorino and some guanciale fat
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Mix pasta with the egg mixture
  6. Add black pepper and extra pecorino on top
