Lasagna

A quick and easy lasagna recipe for your special occasions.

Recipe

When I was young, my mother always told me to start every recipe with

long and unrelated story of my life. Back in 1965...

Ingredients

  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml beef stock

Method

  1. Sear mince
  2. Add prosciutto
  3. Pour passata
  4. Let is simmer for 2/3 hours
