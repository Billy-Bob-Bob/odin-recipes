Spinach and Ricotta Cannelloni

A quick and easy cannelloni recipe for your special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 200g ricotta
  • 150g wilted spinach
  • 250g beschamel
  • 500g dried cannelloni

Method

  1. Mix ricotta with the wilted spinach
  2. Fill the dried cannelloni with the mixture
  3. Add a base layer of beschamel to your tray
  4. Fill tray with your filled cannelloni
  5. Top with the remaining beschamel and add parmesan
  6. Back for 30 minutes or until bubbling
